My Journey in Digital Product

I graduated from the University of Michigan with a degree in Cognitive Science and a minor in UX Research and Design. My career began as a UX Testing Intern at FRICH, a fintech startup, where I gained hands-on experience in user research and testing methodologies. This foundation led me to explore digital transformation at HW Kaufmann, a specialty insurance company working on a SalesForce integration.

I then joined WillowTree Apps as a Product Analyst Intern, where I developed my analytical skills working on mobile applications and websites. Currently, I'm a Product Analyst at TELUS Digital, where I analyze user behavior and product metrics to drive strategic decisions and improve user experiences across digital platforms.
