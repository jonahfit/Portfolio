Case Study 2024

Northeast Airlines Case

Conducted comprehensive analysis of Northeast Airlines' digital transformation challenges, analyzing customer journey data and providing strategic recommendations.

3 Months Duration

Project Overview

The Northeast Airlines project was a comprehensive summer internship assignment where I partnered with another product analyst intern to create a new airline app for a fictional airline called Northeast Airlines. We worked with a simulated client throughout the summer to develop recommendations for a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) that would address real-world airline industry challenges.

This project provided hands-on experience in the complete product development lifecycle, from initial requirements gathering and stakeholder interviews to creating detailed prototypes and strategic recommendations. We conducted extensive research into airline industry pain points and user needs to inform our product decisions.

Project Scope

  • Requirements gathering and stakeholder interviews
  • User research and competitive analysis
  • Feature prioritization and MVP definition
  • Wireframing and prototyping
  • Strategic recommendations and roadmap
  • Comprehensive presentation and documentation

Tools Used

Figma FigmaJam User Research Prototyping Strategy

Team Size

2 people
