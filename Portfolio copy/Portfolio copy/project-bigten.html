As an Associate Producer for the BigTenStudentU program at the University of Michigan, I managed comprehensive sports media production workflows, coordinated student talent, and oversaw digital content creation for campus athletic events. This role provided hands-on experience in live production, content management, and team coordination within a professional media environment while developing leadership skills in managing large-scale production operations.

I worked closely with student athletes, production crews, and network executives to deliver high-quality sports coverage while developing leadership skills in media production and project management. The role involved managing multiple concurrent productions and ensuring seamless execution of live broadcasts and digital content.
