UX Research 2023

UX Research Project

Conducted comprehensive user research study at the University of Michigan, analyzing user behavior patterns and pain points to inform product decisions.

4 Months Duration
5 User Interviews

Project Overview

This semester-long UX research project was conducted as part of my coursework at the University of Michigan. I was tasked with identifying a real problem affecting the university community and conducting comprehensive UX research to understand the root causes and develop potential solutions.

The project involved the complete UX research lifecycle, from initial problem identification through data analysis and solution recommendations. I conducted in-depth user interviews, analyzed qualitative data, and created comprehensive deliverables documenting my findings and recommendations.

Research Focus Areas

  • Problem identification and validation
  • User interview methodology
  • Qualitative data analysis
  • Solution ideation and recommendations
  • Comprehensive documentation

Tools Used

User Interviews Qualitative Analysis Research Methods Documentation

Project Scope

Individual project

  • 1 UX Researcher (me)
  • 5 Interview participants

Research Process

1

Identify Problem

Conducted initial research to identify real problems affecting the university community, validated problem significance, and defined research scope.

2

Write Interview Guide

Developed comprehensive interview guide with open-ended questions, follow-up prompts, and structured methodology for data collection.

3

Recruit Participants

Identified and recruited diverse participants from the university community, ensuring representative sample for meaningful insights.

4

Conduct Interviews

Executed 5 user interviews, gathering qualitative data about user experiences, pain points, and needs related to the identified problem.

5

Code Interviews

Systematically coded interview transcripts, identifying themes, patterns, and key insights using qualitative analysis methods.

6

Analyze Interview Data

Synthesized coded data to identify root causes, user needs, and opportunities for improvement through comprehensive analysis.

7

Create Deliverables

Developed comprehensive research report, recommendations, and presentation materials documenting findings and proposed solutions.
